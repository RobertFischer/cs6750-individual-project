Podcast Intermission Prototype

Is a silent pause the best thing to go between podcast episodes?

This is a prototype that explores the user experience at the moment when one podcast episode shifts to another.

Thanks to the Historical Blindness podcast for collaborating with this project.

Interactive Demo

NOTE: The voice is a text-to-speech voice provided by your browser, so do not factor that into your consideration.

Moving Between Episodes in a Series

In this demo, you will hear the end of one podcast episode in a series and the start of another in the same series, with an intermission voice announcing the next episode.

Moving Between Podcasts

In this demo, you will hear the last episode of one podcast and the start of an episode of a different podcast, with an intermission voice announcing the next podcast and episode.
